Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026. This policy describes how Northrise Digital collects and uses your personal information.

1. Who We Are

Northrise Digital is an Edmonton-based web design agency operating in Alberta, Canada. Contact: hello.northrisedigital@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your business type
  • Project details you choose to share

We also collect basic analytics data (page views, device type, location at the city level) through Google Analytics. This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Provide you with a quote for web design services
  • Communicate about your project if you become a client

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Formspree (formspree.io). Your form submission is transmitted to and stored by Formspree. Please review Formspree's Privacy Policy for details.

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies and may collect your IP address. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months. If you become a client, we retain project-related communications for the duration of our working relationship and up to 2 years after.

6. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your information
  • Request deletion of your information

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello.northrisedigital@gmail.com.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Our website is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are encrypted in transit by Formspree.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello.northrisedigital@gmail.com.
