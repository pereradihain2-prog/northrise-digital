## 18. DO NOT / AVOID LIST

- No purple gradients (looks generic and AI-generated)
- No stock photos of people pointing at laptops or shaking hands
- No "We are passionate about digital solutions" type fluff copy
- No vague CTAs ("Learn More", "Submit", "Click Here")
- No hiding pricing — show it clearly
- No claiming years of experience or fake awards
- No same colors as any of the 3 demos (no greens, no orange, no charcoal-only)
- No `transition: all` in CSS
- Comic Sans, system fonts, or default Tailwind colors
- Forbidden phrases from CLAUDE.md
- Endless animations or scroll effects that hurt performance
